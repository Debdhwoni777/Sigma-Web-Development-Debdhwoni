who is LODE RAM ?

Rama, the Hindu god, is the seventh avatar of the god Vishnu. In Hindu mythology, an avatar is an animal or human form into which a god can incarnate in order to counterbalance some opposing evil. Vishnu has ten avatars, depending on local stories in each area where he is worshiped.21 Apr 2023

who is LODE KRISHNA ?

Lord Krishna is the Hindu god of love, compassion, and protection. Known as the Dark One, he is often depicted with dark blue skin. Krishna is an avatar of the god Vishnu, but he is also worshiped as a god in his own right. Hindu mythology is filled with tales about Krishna's life.
